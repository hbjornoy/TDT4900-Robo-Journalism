the england football team will not use british airways to travel to next year 's world cup in brazil after talks broke down between the airline and the football association .
ba is said to have pulled out of negotiations over the # 10million cost of transporting the players , coaches and entourage to the tournament .
however , the national flagship carrier insists that it simply can not spare a plane for the team during the peak summer season .
