for his 50th birthday , jim greenan was probably expecting a slap-up meal or a romantic break .
instead , all his wife claire potter gave him was a wicker box containing 50 brightly coloured pieces of paper .
on each was written a small challenge to be done in order throughout the year .
they ranged from the routine -lrb- baking a loaf -rrb- , to the daring -lrb- skinny-dipping -rrb- and the bizarre -lrb- talking like a pirate , volunteering for a police identity parade -rrb- .
