a baby meerkat which was expected to die after being abandoned is now being reared by an unusual adoptive mother - a pet chihuahua .
tiny wilson was born on january 20 - but was immediately shunned by his real mother as the runt of the litter .
dennis drew , 30 , owner of mablethorpe seal and animal sanctuary , in lincolnshire had the idea of introducing him to his colleague 's pet chihuahua kimi - who was going through a phantom pregnancy at the time .
she has reared him as her own ever since .
