the nestle prepared foods co. has ordered a recall of lean cuisine mushroom ravioli after consumer reports that glass shards were found in the product .
officials say affected packages of culinary collection mushroom mezzaluna ravioli have productions codes of 2311587812 and 2312587812 .
both codes have a ` best before ' date of december 2013 .
nestle said that it was forced to issue the recall after three people reported finding the glass in their meals .
