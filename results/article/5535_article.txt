ms harrison , who owns willows cat adoption centre in south shields , newcastle upon tyne , serves up coffee and cake with a side of kitten to raise much needed funds for her homeless moggies .
and her cafe is so popular that visitors have to book ahead before turning up for their 45-minute kittie catch-up .
