the number of people with diabetes will jump from 7.3 per cent of the population in 2012 to 8.8 per cent by 2030 .
people aged between 40 and 74 in england are being urged to have an nhs health check to spot the first signs of the disease .
the checks could also highlight certain diets or lifestyles that may lead to diabetes , or spot other illnesses , such as heart disease or kidney failure .
