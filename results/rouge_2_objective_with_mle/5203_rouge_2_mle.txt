police in fort lee , nj used an undercover cop dressed in a donald duck costume to bust more than 100 drivers for failing to yield to a pedestrian .
on oct. 31 , as part of the fort lee police department 's pedestrian decoy program , the officer , dressed in the costume , would step out into the crosswalk as if he were crossing the street .
when drivers failed to stop for the costumed cop , uniformed officers pulled them over farther down the road and gave them a moving violation ticket carrying a $ 230 fine as well as two points on their driver 's license .
