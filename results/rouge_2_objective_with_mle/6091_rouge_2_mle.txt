real madrid manager carlo ancelotti and keeper iker casillas had reason to celebrate in training on wednesday .
ancelotti was marking his 100th game in charge at the bernabeu , while casillas was celebrating his 500th la liga game between the sticks , both of which came during the 2-0 victory away at elche on sunday .
ancelotti also recorded his 78th win in the game against elche to help him eclipse jose mourinho as the most successful real madrid boss .
