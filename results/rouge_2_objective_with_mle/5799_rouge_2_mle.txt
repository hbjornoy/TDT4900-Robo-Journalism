everton striker samuel eto'o is on the verge of signing for serie a side sampdoria .
the genoa outfit has been in talks with everton and eto'o 's representatives in recent weeks with a view to completing the move for the 33-year-old .
the 33-year-old has reportedly reduced his wages by 40 per cent in order to return to serie a year .
the former cameroon striker spent two seasons at inter milan and was a member of the nerazurri 's 2010 treble-winning side that won the serie a title , coppa italia and champions league under then head coach jose mourinho .
