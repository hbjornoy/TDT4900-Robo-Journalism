the renwick ruin on manhattan 's roosevelt island in new york was also known as the smallpox hospital .
it was constructed between 1854 and 1856 and remained open for a century before being abandoned and falling into ruin .
the hospital was eventually designated a new york city landmark in 1976 .
the site is closed off and an ongoing $ 4.5 million renovation project is underway before the ruins open to the public .
