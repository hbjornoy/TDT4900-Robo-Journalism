mohammad siddiqur rahman chowdhury , an islamic studies teacher in bethnal green , is being investigated by the home office after it was revealed he was found guilty of murder in his native bangladesh .
the teacher had undergone a criminal record bureau check -lrb- crb -rrb- but the conviction was not unearthed as the crime had happened overseas .
the case has highlighted a problem with crb checks which can lead to crimes committed overseas going undetected .
