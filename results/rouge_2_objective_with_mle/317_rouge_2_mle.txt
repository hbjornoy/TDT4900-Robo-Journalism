mathew mcgrath , 18 , was arrested after a hoax call to police caused authorities to swoop on his family home in the southern suburb of arncliffe around 4.40 am on wednesday .
he was taken to kogarah police station for questioning but he may have fallen victim to a ` swatting ' prank , whereby callers in fake crimes to elicit an urgent police response to another person 's house .
swatting is a modern equivalent of a bomb hoax by telephone and is carried out using online technology that masks the perpetrator 's identity .
