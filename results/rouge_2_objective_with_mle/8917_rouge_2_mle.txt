james tyrwhitt-drake from the university of victoria in canada has created a high-definition video of planet earth -lrb- still from the video shown -rrb- .
he used images captured by the geostationary elektro-l weather satellite from 15 to 19 may 2011 .
the images were resized by 50 per cent , misalignments between frames were manually corrected , and image artifacts that occurred when the camera was facing towards the sun were partially corrected .
