barry minkow pleaded guilty to embezzling funds from the san diego community bible church over the course of nearly 10 years , a u.s. attorney 's statement said .
minkow , 46 , has been serving a five-year sentence for a securities fraud conviction in florida and could get five additional years when he is sentenced for the new conviction april 7 .
minkow admitted that he opened unauthorized church bank accounts , forged signatures on checks and pocketed member donations for himself .
