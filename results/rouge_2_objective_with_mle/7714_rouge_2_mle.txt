russia 's ekaterina makarova reached her second successive grand slam semi-final on tuesday , defeating third seed simona halep in the last four of the us open .
the no 11 seed makarova , 26 , then cruised through the second set to set up a semi-final with two breaks of serve as halep committed a string of unforced errors .
the 23-year-old romanian did recover one of the breaks to register her first game and held to trail 2-4 .
