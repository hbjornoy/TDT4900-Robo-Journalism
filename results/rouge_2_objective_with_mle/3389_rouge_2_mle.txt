islamic state fighters ambushed an iraqi army convoy of humvees with a bulldozer packed with explosives , killing a commander and three of his soldiers , military officials say .
a suicide bomber first attacked the convoy before militants opened fire , killing the brigadier general hassan abbas toufan , the commander of the iraqi first division , a member of the division and an intelligence officer .
the iraqi army is embroiled in a fierce battle to reconquer the western anbar province , which has been under isis control for the past year .
