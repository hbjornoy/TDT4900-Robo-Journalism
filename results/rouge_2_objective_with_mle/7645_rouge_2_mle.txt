a melbourne school student was seen pinned to a wall by two ticket inspectors at a melbourne train station in melbourne 's south-east .
footage of the incident , which occurred at windsor station , was recorded by witnesses and shows the two metro officers holding down the student from either side over a low brick wall at about 8:30 am .
witnesses told channel nine news that the physical dispute occurred after the teenage boy allegedly evaded the inspectors three times following an apparent disagreement regarding his fare .
