pictures show a traditional indian buffalo race held in padupanambur , a village near mangalore in the state of karnataka .
the pictures were taken at the annual mulki seeme arasu kambala kambala -lrb- slush track buffalo race -rrb- which sees pairs of buffaloes racing around muddy waters .
the sport has a history spanning many centuries in the coastal district and the kambala season generally starts in november and lasts until march .
