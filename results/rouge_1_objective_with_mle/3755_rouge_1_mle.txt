celebrities are posting pictures of themselves to campaign against oil exploration off the coast of ibiza , the mediterranean playground for the rich and famous .
kate moss , singer sophie ellis-bextor , and dj fatboy slim and his wife zoe ball have posted selfies on the internet and signed a petition lobbying the spanish government not to lease territory to oil giant cairn energy .
the petition , which now has 60,000 signatures , will be submitted this week by the ibiza preservation fund , a consortium of businesses and residents .
