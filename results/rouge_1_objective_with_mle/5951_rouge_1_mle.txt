kentucky republican sen. rand paul told an audience in 2010 to block an obamacare rule that only permitted americans to keep their health insurance policies if nothing about them was changed from year to year .
paul told a partisan crowd on nov. 11 that the obama white house crafted the regulation that has forced nearly 5 million americans in the ranks of the uninsured .
paul said senate republicans tried to give insurers a one-year grace period .
