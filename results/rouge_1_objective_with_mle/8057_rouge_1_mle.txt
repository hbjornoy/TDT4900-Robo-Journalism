the ringling bros. and barnum & bailey circus will phase out the show 's iconic elephants from its performances by 2018 .
the decision to end the circus 's century-old tradition of showcasing elephants was difficult and debated at length as elephants have often been featured on ringling 's posters over the decades .
13 animals will continue to tour with the circus before retiring to the company 's 200-acre center for elephant conservation in florida .
