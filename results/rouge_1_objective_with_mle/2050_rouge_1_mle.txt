rangers football board chairman sandy easdale has increased his stake in the club after buying up more than one million shares .
the purchase means he now owns just over 5,250,000 shares - or just less than 6.5 per cent of the club 's total shareholding .
the mcgill 's bus boss previously held proxies for just over 17million shares belonging to a number of other shareholders , including blue pitch holdings and margarita funds holding trust .
he has now called a general meeting to oust the current board of directors which includes easdale 's brother james as well as chairman david somers and mike ashley associates derek llambias and barry leach .
