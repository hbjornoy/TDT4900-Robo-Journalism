private schools are ditching gcses in record numbers in favour of tougher qualifications based on the old o-level .
they are increasingly turning to international gcses , which are seen as better preparation for a-levels despite government reforms to conventional exams .
the number of independent school entries for igcses leapt by 18 per cent this summer to 152,170 -- accounting for 38.7 per cent of year 11 exam entries .
