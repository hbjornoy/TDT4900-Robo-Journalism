conor mcgregor will challenge the ufc featherweight champion in las vegas on july 11 .
the dubliner earned his shot with a second-round stoppage of dennis siver earlier this month and will headline ufc 189 , the fourth annual fight week .
mcgregor has made seven defences of his title and is ranked no 2 in the pound-for-pound list .
mcgregor insisted the fight would simply be the next step on his road to greatness .
