usain bolt will make his first appearance in the united states in five years when he competes in the adidas grand prix diamond league meeting in new york city on june 13 .
the jamaican sprinter set his first 100 metres world record at the meeting in 2008 when he was to anchor a jamaican 4x100 metres team at the 2010 penn relays .
bolt says he is excited to return to new york , a place that holds fine memories after his previous success .
