racing legend tony mccoy ended his career as a jump jockey without adding to his haul of more than 4,300 winners .
mccoy , 40 , has been champion jump jockey for the past 20 years , and when he was handed the trophy for the final time today by former arsenal player ian wright , it emerged that the irish jockey will be allowed to keep it in perpetuity .
mccoy was joined on stage by his wife chanelle and daughter eve and young son archie .
mccoy has ridden in more than 17,000 races and fallen an estimated 1,000 times breaking almost every bone in his body at sandown park .
