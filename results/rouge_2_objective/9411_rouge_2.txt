nasa scientists in california have released new images of the north pole of dwarf planet ceres from the dawn spacecraft .
dawn arrived on 6 march , the first time a spacecraft has orbited a dwarf planet , and is now preparing for the key science phase of the mission .
the images have not yet revealed what the mystery ` bright spots ' on the surface of the world are , which have gripped the attention of scientists and the public alike .
the latest images were taken on 10 april from a distance of 21,000 miles -lrb- 33,000 km -rrb- , and are the highest-resolution views of ceres so far .
