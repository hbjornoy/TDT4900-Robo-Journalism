the loss of the bees will place farming in crisis because of the insects ' vital role in pollinating crops , claim conservationists .
sixteen of 68 bumblebee species in europe are at risk of extinction as their habitats are destroyed , according to the red list of the international union for conservation of nature -lrb- iucn -rrb- .
some 26 of the listed 68 species live in britain , of which 12 are decreasing , six are stable and five increasing .
the iucn species have falling populations and just 13 per cent are increasing .
