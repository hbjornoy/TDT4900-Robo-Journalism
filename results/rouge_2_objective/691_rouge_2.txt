professor miles hewstone , director of the oxford centre for the study of intergroup conflict , said governments could create more ` harmonious neighbourhoods ' by doing more to encourage different ethnic groups to mix .
an international study found that white people develop ` passive tolerance ' of minorities in mixed areas , such as central london -lrb- pictured -rrb- even if they have no direct contact with them .
the oxford university-led study , published in the proceedings of the national academy of sciences -lrb- pnas -rrb- journal , is based on seven studies carried out in england , mainland europe , the united states and south africa between 2002 and 2011 .
