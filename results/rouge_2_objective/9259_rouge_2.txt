british photographer tim plowden captures the diversity and wonder of alaska 's wilderness in his new series of photographs .
plowden , who grew up enjoying the chiltern hills of the south-east and volunteered as a nature reserve warden in norfolk , travelled by boat , plane , four-wheel drive and campervan around the unforgiving state of the us .
plowden 's journey will be food for a young fish or scavenger while a new day dawns over a rocky stream .
