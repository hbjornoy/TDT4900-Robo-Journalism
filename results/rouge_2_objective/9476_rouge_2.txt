swansea striker bafetimbi gomis has been backed to fill the void left by wilfried bony 's departure to the african nations cup .
gomis , who has been linked with a move to crystal palace in the january transfer window , will start his second game in the space of 48 hours in saturday 's fa cup third-round tie at tranmere and swansea boss garry monk expects the 29-year-old frenchman to seize his chance .
gomis has played second fiddle to bony this season since his move from lyon .
