apple 's chief executive tim cook has reported it sold more than 10 million iphone 6 and 6 plus models over the weekend - a three-day record for a new model .
this beat the previous record of 9 million for last year 's iphone 5s and 5c .
the two models went on sale in the us , australia , canada , france , germany , hong kong , japan , puerto rico , singapore and the uk on friday .
the iphone 6 has a 4.7-inch screen , while the new phones offer faster performance and a wireless chip for making credit card payments .
