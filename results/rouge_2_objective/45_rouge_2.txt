tony lepore has become a crossroad sensation in providence , rhode island , after turning his daily car management duties into a highly-choreographed dance routine .
he has been entertaining motorists since 1984 when he came up with the moves to alleviate boredom after he was transferred from night shifts foiling crime to traffic duties .
mr lepore has had his share of serious police work , spending nine years on night duty in providence and being decorated for valour in the line of duty .
