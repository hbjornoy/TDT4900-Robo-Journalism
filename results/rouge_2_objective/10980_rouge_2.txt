oscar winning actor christoph waltz was left shaken this evening after a man pointed a hand-gun at him and threatened to blow up a studio with a fake grenade .
waltz , who won oscars for roles in two quentin tarantino movies , was being interviewed on the set of a live french news programme with fellow actor daniel auteuil when two shots were fired .
waltz was being detained by police and found he was carrying a dummy grenade and a knife , authorities said .
