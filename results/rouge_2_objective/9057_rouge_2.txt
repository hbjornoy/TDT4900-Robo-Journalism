the world is celebrating the 40th anniversary of the first call from a cell phone , which was made in new york city on april 3 , 1973 .
the momentous first call was made by motorola employee marty cooper , who used a prototype known as a dynatac to call rivals bell labs to inform them that they had been beaten in the race to achieve the technological breakthrough .
the motorola dynatac phone can be seen in several hollywood films .
