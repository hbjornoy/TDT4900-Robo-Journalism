terence crawford defeated thomas dulmorme in the sixth round with a sixth-round technical knockout saturday night , knocking down dulorme three times in quick fashion .
crawford was a two-time wbo champion , winning the latest title in his first fight since moving up in class .
crawford won the vacant wbo junior welterweight title 13 months ago and defended in the 135-pound division twice in his hometown of omaha , nebraska .
the scheduled 12-round fight in north texas came a week before crawford , now 26-0 with 18 knockouts , is scheduled to be in new york to accept his 2014 fighter of the year award from the boxing writers association of america .
