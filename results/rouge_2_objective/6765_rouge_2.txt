tampa bay lightning left wing ryan malone was arrested early saturday morning and charged with dui and cocaine possession .
malone , 34 , was pulled over only blocks from the teams arena just before 3.30 a.m. after police saw his suv hit a curb .
cops said they smelled alcohol on his breath and found 1.3 grams of cocaine in one of his back pockets .
malone refused to take field sobriety tests , but a breath test given at the jail recorded blood alcohol levels of 0.112 and 0.116 percent .
