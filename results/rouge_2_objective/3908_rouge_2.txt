the pictures were taken at the annual mulki seeme arasu kambala kambala kambala -lrb- slush track buffalo race -rrb- held in padupanambur , a village near mangalore in the state of karnataka .
india 's famous ` kambala ' features pairs of buffaloes racing through muddy water .
the sport has a history spanning many centuries in the coastal district and the kambala season generally starts in november and lasts until march .
