russia 's soyuz tma-15m rocket is delivering three new crew members to the international space station - including italy 's first female astronaut .
soyuz tma-15m , holding incoming station commander terry virts , with the us space agency nasa , left the baikonur cosmodrome in kazahkstan .
the astronauts were expected to reach the station , which flies about 260 miles above earth , less than six hours after taking off .
the crew face a busy six months in orbit , including a trio of spacewalks to prepare the station for a new fleet of space taxis .
