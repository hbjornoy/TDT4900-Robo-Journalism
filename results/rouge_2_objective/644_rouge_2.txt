three crew members have safely returned to earth after spending 167 days on the international space station .
they returned in a soyuz capsule , landing southeast of the town of zhezkazgan in kazakhstan at 2.07 am gmt -lrb- 8.07 am local time -rrb- this morning .
the soyuz tma-14m -lrb- shown -rrb- took slightly more than three hours to return after leaving the iss .
