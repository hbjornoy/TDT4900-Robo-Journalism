anyone booking a # 999 per person 11-night classic round voyage around norway between 1 october and 31 march next year before 30th november will be offered the deal .
norwegian cruise line hurtigruten are so confident that holidaymakers will be able to witness the lights on one of their voyages that they are now offering visitors a free second trip if they are not visible .
the company have become the first operator to offer such a promise , which coincides with an 11-year period known as ` solar maximum ' by scientists when the lights are more vivid than usual .
