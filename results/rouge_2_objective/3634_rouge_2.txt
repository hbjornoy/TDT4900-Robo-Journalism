lillian weber , 99 , has created more than 840 frocks for a christian nonprofit group called little dresses for africa .
she hopes to make 1,000 dresses for impoverished girls before she turns 100 on may 6 , 2015 .
mrs weber , who lives in a farmhouse in bettendorf , iowa , has received more than 2.5 million dresses from all 50 states , plus the u.k. , ireland , canada , mexico , mexico and australia .
