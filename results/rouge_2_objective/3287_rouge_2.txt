daniel dudzisz was attempting an epic 2,485 mile -lrb- 4,000 km -rrb- journey on foot from new south wales to uluru via queensland .
the 26-year-old became stranded between windorah and jundah in central-west queensland after being cut off by floodwaters .
police inspector mark henderson told the abc mr dudzisz became lost and was stranded in bushland for about 10 days between two flooded sections of the barcoo river .
mr dudzisz was found near coopers creek by two men yesterday afternoon .
