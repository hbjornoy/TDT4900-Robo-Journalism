chinese football authority has announced plans to recruit a first batch of 120 foreign coaches by the end of 2015 to help teach pupils in primary and secondary schools , according to people 's daily online .
the news comes as a part of the ` campus football promotion campaign ' , which is widely understood as the country 's effort to turn the nation into a football powerhouse by raising a generation of all-conquering young footballers .
china 's current president xi jinping is a massive fan of the game and is keen to see his country qualifying for another world cup .
