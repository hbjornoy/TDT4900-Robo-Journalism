denise van outen will cycle 250 miles across vietnam and cambodia in january to raise money for great ormond street hospital .
the former big breakfast presenter has climbed mount kilimanjaro and trekked the great wall of china , and in 2012 completed a 180-mile cycle ride across india to help the hospital .
she will be accompanied on the latest challenge by tv friends lydia bright from the only way is essex and zoe hardman is a poster girl for charity sporting challenges .
