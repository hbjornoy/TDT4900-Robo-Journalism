south cobb high school girls basketball coach melonie joyce martin denies biting the eyes and nose of another coach in an argument over a game .
martin was standing about two inches from coach tyrone harris when authorities say she bit him in the eye and nose , according to an arrest warrant .
martin has denied the allegations , her attorney , s. carlton rouse , is accused of throwing a chair and telling harris she would stomp him and knock his teeth out .
the confrontation happened inside a school office jan. 9 , after the south cobb girls lost to campbell high school , authorities said .
