microsoft chief executive steve ballmer has announced his retirement , ending a 13-year reign at the head of the world 's largest software company and sending the company 's shares up nearly 6 per cent .
ballmer , 57 , took over from co-founder bill gates in january 2000 , but his leadership was questioned throughout his tenure by wall street and silicon valley , as microsoft 's stock price floundered .
ballmer will stay in his position for the next 12 months as the company searches for his replacement .
