the pavlov poke gadget sits under the wrist of a computer user and monitors which sites are used .
pavlov poke is made of an arduino board - a board that can be used to develop applications and service - that is meant to be working , but keeps getting distracted by facebook , for example , or spends too long chatting on forums , the device sends an electric shock .
it was created by ph.d from the massachusetts institute of technology who wanted to reduce the amount of time they spent on sites such as facebook and twitter .
