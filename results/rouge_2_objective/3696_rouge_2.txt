scientists at northeastern university in boston have used air travel information to predict where the deadly ebola virus could spread across the world infecting people from the u.s. to china within three weeks , scientists have warned .
there is a 50 per cent chance a traveller carrying the disease could touch down in the uk by october 24 .
the virus is spreading rapidly across west africa , with more than 3,400 people known to have died and 7,500 infected .
