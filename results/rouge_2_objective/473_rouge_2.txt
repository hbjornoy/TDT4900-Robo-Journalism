princess letizia is famous for more than simply being the wife of the spanish king-in-waiting .
the 41-year-old mother-of-two has also carved out a name for herself as a royal style icon and even , on one memorable occasion , managed to outshine the ultra-glamorous carla bruni .
her current wardrobe of tailored sheath dresses , sky-scraping court shoes and eye-catching jewels is all a far cry from her early days as a newsreader .
