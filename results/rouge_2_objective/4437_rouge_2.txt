u.s and indonesian navy divers have confirmed that a sunken vessel in the java sea is the wreck of the uss houston , a cruiser sunk by the japanese during a ferocious second world war battle .
the ship carried 1,068 crewmen , but only 291 sailors and marines survived both the attack and being prisoners of war .
the houston 's commanding officer , captain albert h rooks , posthumously received the medal of honor for extraordinary heroism .
the wreck has been confirmed as the remains of the houston , nicknamed the galloping ghost of the java coast .
