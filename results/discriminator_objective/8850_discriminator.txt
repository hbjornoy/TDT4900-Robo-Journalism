ian moorhouse has been banned from drinking in kidderminster town centre and nearby bewdley on thursday , friday and saturday evenings under the terms of a criminal anti-social behavior order .
the three-year order also bans moorhouse from using violence towards any person or drinking any form of alcohol in the town centre .
police have urged members of the public to report any sightings of the man breaching the conditions of the order after he was released on december 20 .
