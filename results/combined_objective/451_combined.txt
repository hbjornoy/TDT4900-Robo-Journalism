virgil van dijk has been cleared to play in the qts scottish league cup final after his tannadice red card was rescinded .
the dutchman was sent off in the first half of the scottish cup draw following a clash with calum butcher that resulted in himself and dundee united midfielder paul paton being sent off .
van dijk will now be able to face united at hampden on sunday as well as in the quarter-final replay at celtic park three days later .
