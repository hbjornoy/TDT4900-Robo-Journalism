united airlines said it is notifying passengers who were on flights with a man later diagnosed with ebola and telling them how to contact federal health officials .
it is also telling passengers that officials at the centers for disease control and prevention -lrb- cdc -rrb- believe the man could not have spread the disease during the flights because he was not yet contagious .
the brussels-to-dulles flight was on a boeing 777 that has 266 seats , and the flight to dallas used an airbus a320 with 138 seats .
