blackpool central pier is one of three piers which have been put on the market by owners cuerden leisure for a collective asking price of # 12.6 million .
the historic attraction is the only pier which is grade two listed , meaning any future owners would require listed building consent to make changes to the structure .
but richard baldwin , a director at bilfinger gva , who are selling the piers for cuerden leisure , said it would be very unlikely that a new owner would make significant changes .
