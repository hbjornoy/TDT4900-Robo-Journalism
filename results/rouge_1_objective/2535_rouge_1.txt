michael rapiejko was accused of approaching luis colon as he got out of his car in october 2005 and pointing a gun at him and threatening to shoot him in front of his wife and four children .
the plaintiff , luis colon , also claimed that rapiejko choked him and ordered that he get back into his car .
colon was charged with obstructing governmental administration , resisting arrest and disorderly conduct , all charges that were dropped in april 2006 .
the city settled with colon for $ 20,000 under the condition that he dismiss all other claims .
