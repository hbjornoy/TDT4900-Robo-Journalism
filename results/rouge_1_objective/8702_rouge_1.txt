california red sox player david ortiz is suing a jewellery dealer for selling him substandard jewellery .
the baseball player has filed a civil suit at middlesex superior court in massachusetts after he failed to secure a refund from the businessman .
ortiz claimed that california jeweller randy hamida sold him $ 127,000 for the watch , bracelet and earrings which he said were worth considerably less than he paid for them .
ortiz agreed to pay $ 80,000 along with $ 47,000 worth of his own jewellery to conclude the purchase .
