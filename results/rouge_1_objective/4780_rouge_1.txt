notts county have appointed ricardo moniz as their new manager .
the former tottenham and hamburg coach pulled out of the running due to ` family reasons ' at the end of march but on tuesday moved to meadow lane on a three-year deal .
moniz has six games to save notts county from relegation to league two with the club one point from safety .
the club have confirmed that dave kevan will be moniz 's assistant at meadow lane .
