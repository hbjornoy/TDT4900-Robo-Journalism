bottle of cognac drank by vladimir komarov and yuri gagarin shortly before their deaths will be sold at auction on september 13 .
the cosmonauts enjoyed the bottle of prieure des couleys after learning they would fly pilot and back-up respectively on the soyuz 1 rocket launch in 1967 .
komarov had already become the first man to fly into space on sputnik 1 in 1961 .
the bottle is now being sold for # 2,000 at the german auction house , auctionata .
