bebe said it detected a hacking attack on its payment processing system that could have compromised data from cards swiped in its stores in the u.s. , puerto rico and u.s. virgin islands .
the affected transactions were made between november 8 and november 26 , the day before thanksgiving .
bebe , which was founded in 1976 and headquartered in brisbane , is the latest retailer to fall prey to hackers .
