australian federal police officers are being deployed to the mh17 crash site in ukraine as part of a dutch-led humanitarian mission .
there are 170 afp officers in ukraine with more on the way to join the 11 currently at the crash site .
the officers , who will be unarmed , will search for unrecovered bodies and remains and conduct a forensic examination of the crash .
the prime minister stressed the mission had ` absolutely nothing ' to do with the politics of eastern europe .
the mission is to stay as long as possible to do a professional job but it should take no longer than two to three weeks .
