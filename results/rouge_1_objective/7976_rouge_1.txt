angela spaccia , 55 , was sentenced thursday to nearly 12 years in prison by a judge who called her a con artist and a hog .
spaccia was the first of seven former public officials to be sentenced for their roles in the scandal that cost the small , working-class city more than $ 5.5 million .
spaccia has been found guilty of 11 criminal counts , including misappropriation of public funds , conspiracy and falsification of government records .
