wetherill park pizza maker frank `` mrheli '' cannistra was competing in an australian pro street association meet at sydney dragway on saturday when the throttle in his car gave way .
the 39-year-old recounted the moment , just a second after crossing the finish line of the quarter-mile straight .
he made the split decision to career head-on into the safety wall which sent his car up in a ball of flames .
cannistra , who runs a mobile pizzeria called franco 's mobile pizzeria , has been racing for 20 years and pays for his ` hobby ' with his pizza sales .
