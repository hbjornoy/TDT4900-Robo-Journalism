premier league stars from the past , present and future helped belgium secure a resounding 6-0 victory over lowly andorra in their euro 2016 qualifier .
belgium struck the woodwork three times in 20 minutes , opened the scoring with a penalty from former chelsea midfielder kevin de bruyne on the half-hour mark and then ripped apart andorra 's defence .
tottenham forward nacer chadli doubled the lead with a half-volley and a fine lay-off from divock origi , who will play for liverpool next season , for de bruyne 's second made it 3-0 at halftime .
