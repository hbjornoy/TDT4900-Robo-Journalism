lizzy yarnold has won britain 's first gold medal of the sochi winter olympics in the skeleton .
the former grammar school pupil had dominated the skeleton competition in sochi which saw her hurtle down an ice track at 80mph on a ` tray ' nicknamed mervyn .
she had been cheered on at the track by the ` yarnie army ' -- her former pe teacher mother judith , and her sisters , katie and charlotte .
she was in tears on the podium as she held a valentine 's day card for her boyfriend james roche , who works with the technical team for british bobsleigh .
