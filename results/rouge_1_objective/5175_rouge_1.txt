hms iron duke and its 180-strong crew have docked in portsmouth after a six-month patrol of the south atlantic to the falkland islands .
the ship and its crew protected uk interests abroad , joining allies to develop armed forces training and defending waters from piracy as well as lending humanitarian assistance where necessary .
hundreds of friends and loved ones lined the harbour as the brave servicemen and women returned , having visited 11 countries and travelled more than 27,000 miles .
