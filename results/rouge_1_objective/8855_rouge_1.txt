the princess mary gift boxes were all ready to be sent to the tommies in the trenches 100 years ago .
the cardboard carton containing 80 embossed brass tins in packs of eight was never sent to western front and they remained unopened .
the carton was found in ireland where it was part of a private collection of great war memorabilia .
the 80 embossed princess mary brass tins were found to contain a pencil made from a .303 shell case and a printed note from princess mary .
