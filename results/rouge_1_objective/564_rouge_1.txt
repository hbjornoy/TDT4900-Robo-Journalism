roger baker , 66 , ran his vet practice in whitstable , kent , for 40 years but became overwhelmed by vat debts and inland revenue demands which threatened to close his business .
the father-of-three was found dead in a shed close to his practice on april 19 by his wife and son after they went looking for him when he failed to return home , an inquest heard .
he died from a fatal overdose of a drug usually used to euthanise animals , and cctv footage showed him unlocking the dangerous drugs cabinet and taking out several bottles and syringes prior to his death .
