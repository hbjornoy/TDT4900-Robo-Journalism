panama has detained a north korean-flagged ship after it was found to be carrying undeclared weapons hidden underneath sugar containers , the country 's president says .
panama president ricardo martinelli said the ship , the chong chon gang , was intercepted as it approached the panama canal from cuba and as it was stopped in 2010 in the ukraine .
the ship was pulled over near the port of manzanillo on the atlantic side of the canal .
the undeclared military cargo appeared to include missiles and non-conventional arms .
