finland 's largest airport will be the first in the world to track passengers ' movements in real-time - from the car park to the departure gate .
dozens of sensors throughout helsinki airport will automatically pinpoint the location of mobile phones with wifi setting turned on .
the international airport , which is operated by state-owned finavia and accommodates 15 million fliers a year , estimates 60 to 70 per cent of all visitors will leave their wifi turned on once the system is up and running by the end of the year .
